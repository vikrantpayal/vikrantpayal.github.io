Book : The signal and the noise, Nate Silver

How do you figure out if something is a passing hype or a new pattern that will last? What do you do if the wisest people in the industry start nodding their heads and accepting something that you thought was a fad? (Crypto, ChatGPT) 'The signal and the noise' is a difficult but essential read. Packed with numbers and stories from the past, it attempts to explain how to separate the pivotal moments in history from the deafening tone of talking heads predicting the next big thing.

A very good reference for anyone in a position of authority.
