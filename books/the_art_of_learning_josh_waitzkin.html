Books : The art of learning, Josh Waitzkin



Like most aged progidies, the author seems obsessed with himself. Much of the book is a movie like narration of events in the author's life. In each of these mini-movies, the author emerges the hero. This gets tiring after a while.

The main thing worth taking away from the book is this -
If you want to learn something, immerse yourself into it. Do not learn for the sake of credentials. Do not try to learn some things and assume you can master the subject. If you go deep into one subject, your mind somehow learns to draw parallels with the concepts and lessons in other subjects too. It becomes easier to master multiple different skills.


A nice, easy read. Nothing life altering in my opinion.
