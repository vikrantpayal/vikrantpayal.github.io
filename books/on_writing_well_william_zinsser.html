On Writing Well, William Zensser

Recommended by Venkat Subramaniam, this book is an essential for leaders.



Communication is a big part of leadership. People follow who they relate to. The last few decades have shaped dull, jargon ridden communication as the norm in business communication. Aracane words and long winded sentences used to describe simple concepts. Many influential leaders talk much without saying anything.



Zinsser rips apart these norms with the effortless elegance of a professional butcher. He then proceeds to dispense tips that actually help you convey and convince your audience.



Five stars for this one..
