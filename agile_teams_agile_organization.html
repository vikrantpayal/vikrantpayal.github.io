Agile teams vs. Agile organization



How fast can an organization go if every single development team follows the Agile methodology? The Agile methodology started

years back. The speed at which software gets delivered has, in fact, changed. We release more frequently. We deploy faster. We course correct more. We fail less. I attribute this success more to the dismantling of the draconian 'Project Management' practices than to the Agile methodologies.



Most companies with scrum teams are still not really Agile. The teams may be. Within the bubble of product management, development, devops and SRE, teams usually hit that rhythm. You see deploys. You see releases. Outside that bubble, there is high chance of hitting a wall. Sometimes many walls.
